Welcome to Disability Services

Disability Services is dedicated to providing students with disabilities an equal opportunity to participate in university programs, courses, and activities through reasonable accommodations and services.

About Us

Our office works with the campus community to remove barriers, promote accessibility, and support students in achieving academic success.

Accommodations

Students can register for academic accommodations, review documentation expectations, and learn how accommodations are implemented through the Accommodate system.

Resources

Resources are organized for students, faculty, staff, and families so users can quickly find the information most relevant to them.
